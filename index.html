NPHacks 2025

FAQs

What is a hackathon?
We at NPHacks like to believe that a hack-a-thon is very much like an invent-a-thon where like-minded individuals with various backgrounds and skill sets come together for a weekend to “invent” something from the ground up. The only thing limiting what you build is your creativity — we’ll provide the rest.
Who can attend?
Any high school student can attend! Please note that all attendees are responsible for their own transportation to NPHacks!
Do I have to pay?
VikingHacks is completely free thanks to our sponsors!
What should I create?
You can build whatever you dream of, whether that be a website with an amazing feature, a phone app, or a prototype! Our only rule is that all code and prototypes must be written and made during the hackathon itself. Feel free to use open-source libraries and APIs; companies attending may provide their own as well. Impress us!
What should I bring?
Please bring a valid ID card for admission, a laptop, a charger, and a positive attitude! Additional recommended items are: a refillable water bottle, earphones, any desired peripherals, and some scratch paper for brainstorming. We will provide everything else!
IS NPHacks a virtual or in-person hackathon? What is the schedule like?
VikingHacks 2025 is a one-day in-person event! When applying, please make sure you will be available all day on Saturday, February 22nd, 2025.
When do applications close?
Applications will be announced at a later date.
When will application descisions be released?
Applications will be announced at a later date.
Do i sign up as a team or individual?
You will apply as an individual. We will be personally reviewing each application and accepting on an individual basis.
How will team formations work for admitted students?
You will be able to note down your desired teammates in your application. There can be up to a maximum of 4 hackers on a team. If you are a solo hacker but want to be in a team, please mention so on your application so we can put you in a team.
What if I have no prior experience in programming, coding, or hackathons?
Students of all skill levels are encouraged to attend, even if you've never programmed before. VikingHacks is beginner-friendly and we will host various workshops throughout the day that can help you get started.
Am I allowed to come with a project pre-built or will I be building the project from scratch?
You will be building projects from scratch. You are encouraged brainstorm ideas of what you want to build; however, participants who begin creating projects before the hacking period begins will be immediately disqualified from the competition.
I have another question!
Please email us at team@nphack.club if you have any additional questions! We look forward to hearing from you.
